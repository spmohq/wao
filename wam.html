I wanted to share a learning tool that may help your students when a topic feels difficult.

Sometimes the challenge isn’t the topic itself, but a few missing foundational ideas. Once those basics are learned, the main topic becomes clearer.

Here’s a simple method:

1. Visit https://spm.in.net/
2. Enter age + language
3. Type the topic
4. Paste the generated prompt into ChatGPT

It provides missing prerequisite concepts, a short study plan, and a clearer explanation of the topic.

The tool is simple enough that teachers, parents, and students can all use it.
