I wanted to share a learning resource that may be helpful for your students, especially when they find a topic difficult even after sincere effort.

Often, students struggle not because the topic is too advanced, but because a few essential foundational ideas are missing. When those basics are clarified, the main topic becomes much easier to understand.

Here is a simple method that helps students discover and learn the missing foundational concepts:

1. Visit https://spm.in.net/
2. Enter the student’s age and preferred language
3. Type the topic they want to understand
4. Copy the prompt generated by the app and paste it into ChatGPT

This provides:
- A clear list of the prerequisite concepts the student may be missing
- A short, focused study plan
- A simpler and clearer explanation of the topic itself

The tool is designed so that teachers, parents, and students can all use it to make learning smoother and more accessible.

If this helps even one student feel more confident and makes learning lighter, it is worth sharing. If not, no worries at all — I simply wanted to pass along a resource that many learners have found helpful.
